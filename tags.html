Basic Care

Taking care of your fish is a very easy task. Once you know what you are doing.
So stop asking yourself Why are all my fish dying? Getting the proper equitment
is very important in order for your fish to thrive.

Supplies

A lot of items are needed for a fish to thrive. Many different fish need different enviorments to survive.
Just because a fish can fit does not mean that is the best fit for the fish to live in.

  • aquarium
  • substrate
  • conditioner
  • things for the fish to hide in
  • plants
  • food

By no means is this everything you need but this is a great start for making your fishy a home.
A great place to go to get all these things is petsmart or petco.

Cycle Tank

This is probably the most important from your success with fish living in a fish tank.
This is based on bactieria in the tank that can transform the bad bacteria that can kill your fish into
good bacteria that helps them thrive. Otherwise known as the nitrogen cycle.

  1. Get a bowl small enough to fit through the top of the tank
  2. Began scooping our water until you have taken 25% of the water out of the tank
  3. Now began to fill the bowl up with water and condition the water based on the directions on the back of the conditioner
  4. After conditioning the water slowly pour the water back into the tank till it is full again
  5. And that is it! Now you have clean water and happy fish
    6. By doing these steps you will find that your fish are healthy and happy. Waiting a week after a tank being set up is another
    really important thing to do.
