Betta fish care is very easy once you understand what you are doing. Once you know how to take care of a betta you can apply this most fish in the industry! Having a betta can be a very rewarding and beautiful pet. However, this animal still has needs and has to be cared for.


The most common sex of betta that people get are males. They are a beautiful choice but they do come with their own struggles and challenges. Males bettas are much more aggressive than the females but they also come with much more elaborate fins. Due to these fins you have to be careful about the filter used. A low flow filter is key to keepiing these fins healthy and pretty.

Weekly betta care

  • Feeding 4 times a week
  • Water changes
  • Testing water waste levels
